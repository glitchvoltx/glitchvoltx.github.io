Who and what is GlitchVoltx?
GlitchVoltx is an indie studio dedicated to the intersection of code, music, and anime aesthetics. From building custom local music players with AI-powered lyrics to producing melancholic rom-com soundtracks, we bridge the gap between technical development and creative storytelling.

Where does GlitchVoltx originate from?

From Egypt.
Bringing Middle Eastern indie creativity to the global anime scene. Proving that world-class games, animation, and music production can come from anywhere with passion and dedication.

Can independent creators or musicians collaborate with GlitchVoltx?

Sadly, the answer is no.
